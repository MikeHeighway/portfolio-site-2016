l'arosteguy

Client: Private
Year: 2016
Disciplines: Brand Identity, Logo Design, Packaging Design, Product Visualization

Arosteguy is a packaging exploration project. With its initial runs being relatively small batch, Arosteguy will be targeting only select retailers and restaurants across the European market, with the target launch date planned for October of 2017.

Label Design

The client wanted some extra “show” for his product. The label had to be unique. An hommage to the old world label designs, but new, clean and a little bit showy. This design utilized a heavy stock to laminate and employ an edge paint. The result is a clean and memorable trim.
