hewlett packard

Client: Hewlett Packard
Year: 2012-2013
Disciplines: Art Direction, 3D Modeling, Architectural Visualization, Product Visualization

Hewlett Packard is a large company with a diverse product line. I have worked closely with their team on several projects that required high-end architectural and product visualization.

Cloud Computing Center

Hewlett Packard was developing marketing and sales material for their cloud computing centers. I worked closely with several teams within HP to generate architectural and product models. Working from a series of images and line drawings, I modeled and rendered the entire cloud computing ecosystem. This project uses entirely Cinema 4D and the Advanced Render 3 render engine.

Offset Printing Press

Hewlett Packard has an offset printing press. They required similar renderings to above for other marketing and sales materials.
