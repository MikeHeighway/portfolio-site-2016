fontwear

Client: Self
Year: 2014-2016
Disciplines: Art Direction, Brand Identity, Print Design

Fontwear is a project I conceived of in late 2013. It focuses on typographic apparel for lovers of type design and typography. The line will start with epononymous hats, with the embroidered logo and extend into screen printed t-shirts. The line will be designed and printed in Denver, Colorado and use entirely US made apparel. Still under development, fontwear will hopefully hit the market before the end of 2016.
